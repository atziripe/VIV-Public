# VIV Roadmap

This roadmap reflects our current direction. Dates are estimates and subject to change.

---

## ✅ Shipped

- AI-powered personalized training plans
- Adaptive rule engine (strength, cardio, recovery)
- Personalized push notifications
- Workout history and progress tracking
- Email authentication

---

## 🚧 In Progress

- **Nutrition module** — food recommendations integrated with the training plan
- **Dark mode** — full support on iOS and Android
- **Onboarding improvements** — more guided flow for new users

---

## 🗓️ Coming Soon (Q3–Q4 2026)

- **Wearables integration** — Apple Health, Google Fit, Garmin
- **Active recovery plan** — automatic deload weeks based on accumulated fatigue
- **Community** — shared challenges and achievements between users
- **Home screen widget** — daily plan summary without opening the app

---

## 💭 Under Consideration

- Web version (dashboard)
- Multi-device sync
- Couples / group plans
- Live coaching (sessions with real trainers)
